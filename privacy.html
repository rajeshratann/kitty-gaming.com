Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we keep it safe.

Last updated: June 8, 2026
👤

Who We Are

Kitty Gaming is a mobile card-game platform developed and operated by Kirosys. This application ("App") is available on Google Play Store and provides virtual card games including Teen Patti, Call Break, 28, and Kitty.

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the Kitty Gaming mobile application. By using the App you agree to the practices described here.

📦

Data We Collect

Category Data Source
Account Name, email address, profile photo Google Sign-In / Facebook Login / manual entry
Game activity Game history, win/loss records, coin balance, streak data Stored locally on your device (SQLite)
Device Device type, OS version, app version, crash logs Firebase Crashlytics / Analytics
Photos Profile image selected from camera or gallery Only when you explicitly choose to upload one
Ad interactions Ad impressions, clicks, reward status Google AdMob SDK
Network Connectivity state (online/offline) System API — not transmitted

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, precise location, or any real-money transaction data. All in-app coins are virtual and have no monetary value.

🎯

How We Use Your Data

  • Account authentication — log you in securely via Google or Facebook
  • Profile display — show your name and avatar in the app
  • Game experience — track your coins, streaks, history, and leaderboard rank
  • Personalisation — remember your sound, theme, and bot-difficulty settings
  • Advertising — show relevant ads via Google AdMob to support free gameplay
  • App improvement — analyse crash reports and usage patterns to fix bugs
  • Customer support — respond to your queries sent to our support email
🔗

Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party SDKs, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, crash reporting, analytics
  • Google Sign-In — OAuth 2.0 sign-in; governed by Google's Privacy Policy
  • Facebook Login — OAuth sign-in; governed by Meta's Data Policy
  • Google AdMob — ad delivery and measurement; may use device advertising ID
  • Flutter audioplayers — local audio playback only; no data transmitted

We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

📍

Data Storage & Security

Game history, settings, and coin balance are stored locally on your device using SQLite. This data never leaves your phone unless you explicitly share it.

Authentication tokens (Google/Facebook) are managed by the respective SDKs using secure OS keychain storage.

We use industry-standard security measures on Firebase servers. No system is 100% secure; if you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately.

🎂

Children's Privacy

Kitty Gaming is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

⚖️

Your Rights

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of ads — use your device's "Opt out of Ads Personalisation" setting
  • Disconnect social login — revoke app access in your Google or Facebook account settings at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@kitty-gaming.com.
