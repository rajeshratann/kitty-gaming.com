How to delete your account

  1. Open the Kitty app on your device.
  2. Tap your profile avatar in the top corner of the dashboard.
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the Profile screen.
  4. Tap "Delete Account".
  5. Read the confirmation message and tap "Delete Forever".

Your account will be deleted immediately. You will be signed out and redirected to the login screen.

⚠️ This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be erased.

Can't access the app?

If you are unable to open the app, email us at support@kittygaming.in with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the name or email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 business days.
